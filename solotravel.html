6 reasons to go solo

What's so good about being alone?

​

You’ll see incredible places and meet new people. While travelling you’ll have a lot of time alone to think. You will experience new situations and learn about yourself in how you react to them

You will live a story worth telling. Don’t settle if you think life is getting mundane. Travelling is the best way to totally change your life if you feel it’s stagnating.

You’ll get to know yourself better. While travelling you’ll have a lot of time alone to think. You will experience new situations and learn about yourself in how you react to them.

It makes planning the trip a lot easier. We all have places we want to go, and everyone has different schedules. Alone, you don’t have to restrict yourself to other people’s time limits or travel to places that you don’t feel comfortable visiting.

It will help you feel more confident. It will help you feel more confident. Travelling alone can be a daunting prospect. If you can travel alone you have already achieved something to be proud of.

It will make you a better traveller. If you’re alone, you will observe places and people more thoroughly. Being an outsider changes the way you interact with other people. More alone time will make you more reflective.
